ABOUT HACKANIX




Redefining the hackathon experience, HACKANIX creates a portal for youth to enjoy coding while having heaps of fun. It's a chance to solve real problems in the country doing what you like - brainstorm till your brain hurts, come up with a business model and show the world how you'll solve it!

And while going at it, why not have fun? Enjoy the moment with musical performances, games (minus the keyboards, duh), AIESEC dances and finish it off in style with a DJ! Plus, learn more about the industry with guest speeches and see where you can go!

What's for the winners? Erm, CASH!? Walk up to the stage at an awards ceremony like you've never seen before. And then it's the after-party!

    How to sign up? Easy,

    build a team limited to 5 people,
    including at least one school student (or someone just after A/Ls) and one girl.
    Yeah, you can bring a school girl. ;-)

But make sure you have someone with some sense of marketing, selling what you invented, is a part of success.

We'll be updating our website with further details in the coming days, so stay tuned!
